Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

About BBA

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is one of the most popular business-related degree programs. It focuses on management, accounting, finance, marketing, human resource management and entrepreneurship.

Students develop leadership, communication and decision-making skills that help them succeed in the corporate world and business sectors.

Career Opportunities

  • Business Manager
  • Marketing Executive
  • Financial Analyst
  • Human Resource Manager
  • Bank Officer
  • Entrepreneur
  • Corporate Executive
