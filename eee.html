Electrical & Electronic Engineering (EEE)

About EEE

Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) is a branch of engineering that deals with electricity, electronics, communication systems, power generation and modern technologies.

Students learn circuit analysis, electrical machines, power systems, digital electronics, microprocessors, telecommunications and renewable energy technologies.

Career Opportunities

  • Electrical Engineer
  • Power System Engineer
  • Electronics Engineer
  • Telecommunication Engineer
  • Control System Engineer
  • Embedded Systems Engineer
  • Research & Development Engineer
