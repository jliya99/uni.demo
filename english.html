Department of English

About English Department

The Department of English focuses on English Language, Literature, Linguistics, Communication Skills and Creative Writing. Students develop strong reading, writing, speaking and analytical skills.

The program helps students understand world literature, improve professional communication and prepare for careers in education, media, publishing and corporate sectors.

Career Opportunities

  • Teacher / Lecturer
  • Content Writer
  • Journalist
  • Editor
  • Translator
  • Public Relations Officer
  • Corporate Communication Executive
